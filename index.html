The Program

How the intensive
actually works.

Pre-Trip
Weeks Before Departure

Preparation Sessions

Virtual sessions to review the school list, align on strategy, and begin Personal Statement brainstorming. By the time your student boards, the game plan is already in place — no time wasted on arrival.

2 Hours Included
Day 1 · 3 hrs
Discovery, Strategy & Narrative Design

Orientation & Personal Statement Launch

Orientation, school list review, and Personal Statement brainstorming. One counselor works on the essay outline with the student; the other begins activities and honors. The student drafts the first Personal Statement after dinner — momentum from day one.

Day 2 · 6 hrs
Writing the Core Application

Personal Statement Finalized & Resume Built

Personal Statement reviewed, revised, and refined to final. Resume built and polished. Supplements organized school by school. Full-group sign-off, followed by a strategy session to plan topics and identify reusable essays across schools.

Day 3 · 6 hrs
Full Supplement Development

Concentrated Supplement Drafting

Concentrated drafting through the first high-volume school sets. One counselor drafts with the student in real time; the other edits completed pieces and prepares the next school's prompts — a two-track system that maximizes output without sacrificing quality.

Day 4 · 6 hrs
Continued Drafting & Early Audit

Remaining Schools & Consistency Pass

Remaining core schools drafted and completed. A light audit begins for repetition, missing prompts, and school-specific detail. The student does a consistency pass after dinner while the counselors prepare the final review materials.

Day 5 · 3 hrs
Final Review & Handoff

Audit, Reader Pass & Departure

Full application audit, final admissions reader pass, and formatting check. A post-trip completion timeline is built school by school. Follow-up checklist prepared. The student departs with everything in order — and the hardest part behind them.

Post-Trip
After You Return Home

Follow-On Hours with Katie & Andy

Ten additional hours with your counselors to finalize remaining schools, review final drafts, prepare for interviews, and ensure every piece is the best it can be before submission. The relationship doesn't end when the trip does.

10 Hours Included
