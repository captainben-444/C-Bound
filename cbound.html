What Is C-Bound

Where the application

C-Bound is a one-of-a-kind college counseling experience set aboard luxury yachts in the Bahamas. We partner expert college admissions counselors with your student in an intimate, focused environment that simply doesn't exist anywhere else.

In five days, your student will make extraordinary progress on their college application — essays drafted, school list finalized, strategy locked — surrounded by crystal water, island beauty, and unwavering expert attention.

The Program

From first call to
final submission.

Pre-Trip
Weeks Before Departure

Preparation Sessions

Your counselors connect virtually with your student to understand their academic profile, interests, and dream schools. We develop a clear roadmap for the five days — which essays to tackle, what research to complete, and how to make every hour count.

2 Hours Included
Day 1
Arrival Day

Welcome to the Bahamas

Arrive in Nassau, meet your counselors and crew. Board the yacht, settle in, and enjoy a welcome dinner as you depart the harbour. An informal evening session sets the tone — goals, intentions, and the beginning of your student's story.

Days 2–3
Deep Work at Sea

Essays, School List & Strategy

Morning and afternoon counseling sessions at a 2:1 ratio. Common App essay drafted and refined, supplemental essay frameworks built, school list finalized with strategic positioning. The unhurried pace of life at sea creates focus that's impossible to replicate at home.

Days 4–5
Refinement & Exploration

Polish, Activities & Island Life

Counseling sessions continue as the application takes final shape. Activity descriptions written, extracurricular narrative solidified. Afternoons free for island adventures — the very experiences that may become the final flourish in an essay or interview story.

Post-Trip
After You Return Home

Final Review & Submission

Your counselors stay with you. Eight hours of continued support to finalize remaining essays, conduct final reviews, prepare for interviews, and ensure every piece of the application is the best it can possibly be before submission day.

8 Hours Included
